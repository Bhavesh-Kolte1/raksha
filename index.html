About Dr. Raksha Kadam

Dr. Raksha Kadam is a dedicated psychologist with a passion for helping young minds thrive. Specializing in helping students navigate academic pressure and peer relationships, she provides a safe, non-judgmental space where you can explore your thoughts and feelings.

With years of experience working with students and young adults, Dr. Kadam understands the unique challenges faced by today's youth. Her approach combines evidence-based therapeutic techniques with genuine compassion and understanding.

Whether you're struggling with exam stress, self-doubt, or navigating complex social dynamics, Dr. Kadam is here to support you on your journey toward mental wellness and personal growth.
